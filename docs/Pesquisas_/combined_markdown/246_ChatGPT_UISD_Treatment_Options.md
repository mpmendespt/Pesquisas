3. Vasodilators: Certain medications that help dilate blood vessels, such as pentoxifylline or vasodilator injections, have been used in some cases of UISD. They aim to improve blood flow to the inner ear and enhance the recovery process.

In cases where a viral cause is suspected, antiviral medications like valacyclovir may be prescribed to target the potential viral infection.

6. Tinnitus Management: If tinnitus (ringing in the ears) is a significant issue, tinnitus management strategies and therapies may be beneficial in reducing its impact.
