When it comes to sudden-onset hearing loss (SOHL), prompt medical attention is crucial, as early intervention may improve the chances of recovery. The initial evaluation and management typically involve the following steps:

  2. Diagnostic evaluation: The healthcare professional will perform a thorough examination, including a review of your medical history and a physical examination.   3. Corticosteroids: In most cases of SOHL, especially when the cause is unknown (idiopathic sudden sensorineural hearing loss), corticosteroids are often the first-line treatment. They can be administered orally (systemic) or injected directly into the middle ear (intratympanic). The choice of administration depends on various factors and will be determined by the healthcare professional.

  4. Antiviral medication (if applicable): If the sudden hearing loss is suspected to be caused by a viral infection, antiviral medications may be considered. These medications are typically prescribed for SOHL associated with viral conditions like herpes simplex virus (HSV).

  5. Supportive measures: Alongside medical treatment, supportive measures can help facilitate recovery. These may include rest, avoiding exposure to loud noises, managing stress levels, and adopting healthy lifestyle habits.

It‚Äôs important to note that the specific treatment approach may vary depending on the individual case and the underlying cause of the sudden hearing loss.             user_input = values["-INPUT-"].strip()
            print(f"You: {user_input}")
            response = generate_response(user_input)
            print(f"ChatGPT: {response}\n")

    window.close()

if __name__ == "__main__":
    main()
